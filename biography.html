Birth and Early Life

Snorri was born to Sjulfar Yngvarsson and Siggi Mikkilsdottir in approximately the year 832 AD, in a small village in southern Sweden. It was said that all the moose in the land gathered to see him, and speak their blessings over him. Snorri could ski and drink a whole mug of beer by the time he was six years old, and could drink a whole barrel by thirteen. It was around this time that he began conversing with moose, and learning all their wise secrets.



Snorri's Career

Snorri first went viking in his seventeenth year. It was said that he traveled across the sea not in a drakkar, but on the back of a moose. He killed the most Saxons and took the most gold of anyone that year. Snorri would summon moose to his side by shouting in their tongue, and make glorious battle with the Christians. His other deeds include, but are not limited to:

  • Riding to the North Pole on a moose
  • Spending the most time in a sauna (154 days)
  • Growing a magnificent mane of hair
  • Eating more surstromming than anyone in history


The Death of Snorri Sjulfarsson

It was in his eightieth year when Snorri fell off of a table while dancing, and sadly broke his neck. It was a truly pitiful end to the life of a great man. They say that the moose came and carried his body away in their antlers, though no one knows where they brought him.
